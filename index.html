The Taste café

Our brand new café, now open, is truly a taste of wonder for everyone. With both vegan and halal options, we have strived to be able to cater to each and every one of our costumers. An extended drink menu is also in the works to make everything we can offer all the more explosive. The café is open for daily visits every single day, with freshly baked goods and the most delicious coffee in the city! Our café kitchen is also available for catering both small scale and large events.

Meetings rooms

A special meeting coming along? Simply want a change of pace, a new environment? Our meeting rooms are now available for booking! We can offer several types of meeting rooms depending on the amount of people that you want to room - too many and you might want want to consider our seminar rooms. Meeting rooms come with the equipment needed to show videos, presentations, hold pitches and even a show your briefings on our brand-new drawing boards. They're all well lit to promote a good working environment, and food from our The Taste café can be ordered as refreshments and brought up during the meeting, both on impulse and preplanned. Rooms can be booked online, in person and on phone.

Party halls / rroms

Been desperately looking for the perfect place to have the best moment of your life?Well, the wait is over! The taste now brings you our brand new bookable party halls! Our halls are super flexible and can be decorated and morphed into your dream scene, for each and every occasion. We different rooms are available for birthday parties and smaller gatherings, while our grand party hall is designed especially to be booked for large gatherings and wedding receptions, catering from our wonderful café included. Equipment for hiring in bands or djs is also ready to go! Rooms can be booked online, in person and on phone.

Seminar hall

Large seminar hall available to rent by for large corporal and academic gatherings. There is only one large seminar hall and it can room around 300 people, and is fully equipped with projectors, audio systems, dynamic lights and high-end microphones for all occasions. This location can be doubled as an entertainment hall if needed. Note that in contrast to the other rooms and halls, the seminar hall does not offer space a space to eat and therefore catering is not available in the room itself - the café is always open for order, though. The seminar hall can be booked online, in person and on phone.
